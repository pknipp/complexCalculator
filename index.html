  • After ...herokuapp.com above you should type /json/ followed by your complex expression. (ED NOTE: json is not yet available.) If you want it in html rather than json, simply omit /json from the address.
  • Syntax:
    • Spaces are allowed - but discouraged - in whichever format you use, because a %20 will replace each space after you hit return, thereby making the address uglier.
    • numbers:
      • real: This should be an integer or decimal but not as fraction, because "/" has special meaning in a URL. Do not include a comma in any number (even one exceeding one thousand).
      • imaginary: This should be either i or j.
    • operations:
      • addition: +
      • subtraction: -
      • multiplication: Do this either by * or in an implied manner (ie with nothing separating the two numbers).
      • division: Because "/" has special meaning in a URL, the should instead be represented either by div or DIV, or even d or D.
      • exponentiation: ** (recommended) or ^ (OK). The latter is discouraged because each instance of ^ will be replaced by %5E after you hit return
